# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want this repository to be a welcoming place for learners, interview candidates, and contributors of all experience levels.

## Expected behavior

- Be respectful and constructive
- Assume positive intent
- Give feedback that helps others improve
- Keep discussions focused on the code and learning material
- Be patient with beginners and newcomers

## Unacceptable behavior

- Personal attacks or harassment
- Derogatory language
- Dismissing questions without explanation
- Spam, trolling, or deliberate disruption
- Sharing unsafe or copyrighted material without permission

## Enforcement

If a discussion becomes unproductive or harmful, maintainers may remove comments, close issues, or limit participation in the repository.

## Reporting

If you encounter a problem, open an issue or contact the maintainers through the repository's standard communication channel.
